## Competitors Researched

### 1. Guardio (guard.io)
- Browser extension (Chrome/Edge) + mobile
- Blocks malicious sites, phishing, bad extensions
- Data breach alerts
- **Does NOT have:** antivirus, VPN, credit monitoring, messaging protection
- Price: ~$9.99/month individual
- Free plan: almost useless by design

### 2. Aura (aura.com)
- All-in-one: identity theft, credit monitoring, antivirus, VPN, password manager
- 3-bureau credit monitoring on all plans
- $1M identity theft insurance
- Family plan: up to 5 adults + unlimited kids
- **Does NOT have:** strong browser extension, messaging scam protection
- **Problems:** US-focused, expensive ($37+/month family), some customer service issues

## Elder Protection Segment

### Existing players:
- ZoraSafe, SeniorShield.ai, True Link, EverSafe, Deepcover

### Key insight:
The real customer is the adult child (35-50), not the elderly parent.
Pain: anxiety + guilt. Product must be invisible to grandparent, simple for the child.

---

## AI Voice / Deepfake Detection — Why Market is Empty

1. Technically hard — real-time detection with <1s latency
2. Recently became relevant — ElevenLabs mainstream 2023
3. iOS blocks call interception apps
4. False positive risk — high liability
5. Demand awareness gap
